# GhostESP Brand Usage Guidelines

## Background

GhostESP: Revival is the actively maintained continuation of the original Ghost ESP project. We adopted and continued the name as **GhostESP** (also referred to as **GhostESP: Revival**) when we forked and revived the project.

The GhostESP logo, the "GhostESP: Revival" identity, and all original artwork in this repository are the intellectual property of the current project maintainers. The underlying firmware is licensed under GPL-3.0.

These guidelines exist to keep things fair and honest. We're a community-driven open-source project and we want people to build cool things with GhostESP, including selling hardware, but we need to protect the project's identity.

## The Short Version

- **Use the name and logo freely** to describe compatibility (e.g., "runs GhostESP").
- **Do NOT name your product "GhostESP".** You must not use our name as your product name.
- **Don't pretend to be us.** Make it clear you're a third-party seller.
- **Don't use our branding on something that isn't actually running GhostESP.**

## Selling Hardware: Naming Rules

We love that people are selling boards, but **you cannot name your product "GhostESP"**. This confuses users into thinking it is an official product.

**Allowed (Description/Compatibility):**
- "DevBoard **running GhostESP**"
- "WiFi Board **for GhostESP**"
- "GhostESP **Compatible** Stick"
- "Pre-flashed with **GhostESP**"

**Not Allowed (Product Name):**
- "**GhostESP** Board"
- "**GhostESP** Stick"
- "**GhostESP** V2"
- "The **GhostESP**"

If you sell hardware, you must make it clear that your product is *compatible with* our software, not *produced by* us.

## Become an Official Partner

We offer an **Official Hardware Partner** program for vendors who support the project. Partners receive:
- Written permission to use the "GhostESP" name and official logo on products.
- Listing on our official website.
- Priority support and updates.

If you are interested in becoming a partner, please contact us on [Discord](https://discord.gg/5cyNmUMgwh) or email `partners@ghostesp.net`.

## Using the Logo and Wordmarks

The GhostESP logo, the "GhostESP" handwriting wordmarks, and associated artwork are our copyrighted assets. You may use them **only** if you follow these rules:

1. **Non-Primary:** The logo/wordmark cannot be the largest or most prominent element on your packaging or board branding. Your own brand/logo should be primary.
2. **No Modifications:** Do not alter, stretch, or recolor the designs.
3. **No Confusion:** Use of the assets must not imply that your product is the "official" GhostESP hardware.

## Content Creators & Press

Cover GhostESP however you like. Tutorials, reviews, teardowns, comparisons, no worries. See the [press kit](press%20kit/) for high-res assets. Credit "GhostESP: Revival" and link to [ghostesp.net](https://ghostesp.net).

## Community Projects & Forks

Forks are encouraged under the GPL-3.0 license. However, if you fork the project, **you may not use the GhostESP name or Logo** for your fork's branding if it diverges significantly. You should choose a new name and state "Based on GhostESP".

## What We Will Enforce

We reserve the right to take action (including copyright takedowns) against:

- **Impersonation:** Pretending to be an official GhostESP store or maintainer.
- **Passing Off:** Using "GhostESP" as a product name in a way that misleads consumers.
- **GPL Violations:** Selling the firmware without providing source code.
- **Copyright Infringement:** Using our logo or artwork on unauthorized merchandise or unrelated products.
