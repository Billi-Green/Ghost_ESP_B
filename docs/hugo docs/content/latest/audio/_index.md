GhostESP audio playback streams MP3 files between the two chips in **The Banshee** over GhostLink, with output through the TLV320DAC3100 audio hardware.

> **Hardware/build limitation:** Audio playback is currently only available on **The Banshee**. The Banshee uses the `somethingsomething` and `somethingsomething2` builds for its two-chip audio setup. Other builds will not show the Audio app or cannot receive audio output.
